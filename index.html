Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

Welcome to Party Tycoon: Business Empire. Your privacy is important to us at Aqal Games. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application on Android.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a functional and optimized gaming experience, we collect the following limited information:

  • Device Identifiers: Specifically, your Device Advertisement ID (GAID) to help with marketing and attribution.
  • Device Information: Your device name and model to ensure the game runs correctly on your specific hardware.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the game (e.g., session length, levels completed).

2. Third-Party Services

We work with several third-party partners to provide analytics, attribution, and advertising services. These partners may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Adjust: Used for mobile attribution and tracking marketing performance.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for analytics, crash reporting, and backend stability.
  • Pushwoosh: Used to send and manage push notifications.
  • CAS.AI: Used for ad mediation and monetization.
  • Facebook SDK: Used for social features and ad attribution.

3. In-App Purchases (IAP)

Our game includes in-app purchases. All financial transactions are processed securely by the Google Play Store. Aqal Games does not store or have access to your credit card or bank account information.

4. Children's Privacy

Party Tycoon: Business Empire is intended for a mature audience. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

5. Data Retention

We retain collected information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

6. Your Rights

Under regulations like GDPR or CCPA, you may have the right to access, update, or delete the data we have collected. Since we primarily use device-based IDs, you can often reset these identifiers in your Android system settings under "Privacy" > "Ads".
